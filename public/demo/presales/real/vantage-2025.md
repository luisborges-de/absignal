VANTAGE DATA CENTERS ISSUER
Secured Data Center Revenue Term Notes

Sourced from public Morningstar DBRS and Scope Ratings reports on Vantage Data Centers'
securitization program. Figures below reflect publicly disclosed structural features of
the Vantage notes program; the U.S. collateral is anchored in Santa Clara (Silicon Valley).

TRANSACTION SUMMARY
Sponsor / Manager: Vantage Data Centers. Morningstar DBRS assigned a provisional credit
rating of A (low) (sf) to the Class A-2 notes. The Class A notes have an Anticipated
Repayment Date in 2030, five years after closing. The Class A-1 notes have an Anticipated
Repayment Date in 2027, two years after closing, which can be extended twice by one year.
Total issuance of approximately $1,350,000,000. Closing date February 2025.

COLLATERAL POOL
The collateral comprises wholesale and hyperscale data centers anchored in Santa Clara,
California (Silicon Valley), with additional capacity in adjacent U.S. markets,
representing approximately 620 megawatts (MW) of power capacity. Class B notes are
expected to be issued at closing, sized to an average Class A DSCR greater than or equal
to 1.60x and a Class A loan-to-value of 70% or lower.

CASH FLOW WATERFALL AND PERFORMANCE TRIGGERS
Section 1 — Cash Trap. A Cash Trap Event occurs if the debt service coverage ratio (DSCR)
falls below 1.35x at any calculation date, and all excess cash flow is diverted to the
Cash Trap Reserve.

Section 2 — Class A DSCR Covenant. The structure maintains an average Class A DSCR of at
least 1.60x; a deterioration below this level constrains additional issuance.

Section 3 — Class A LTV Test. A Class A LTV Test Event occurs if the Class A loan-to-value
ratio exceeds 70.0%, after which available amounts are applied to mandatory deleveraging.

Section 4 — Rapid Amortization. A Rapid Amortization Event occurs upon a manager
termination event or a sustained DSCR shortfall, redirecting all cash to principal.

Section 5 — Senior Interest Reserve. The issuer maintains a senior interest reserve
account sized to approximately six months of senior interest.
